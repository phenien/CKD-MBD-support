2. Data Collection and Storage

2.1 Health & Fitness Data

What we collect:

  • Patient lab values (calcium, phosphate, PTH, vitamin D)
  • Patient identifiers you enter (initials, ID numbers)
  • Calculation results and clinical notes

How we use it:

  • To perform clinical calculations and provide treatment recommendations
  • To track patient data over time
  • To generate PDF reports

Where it's stored:

  • Locally on your device only
  • Optionally in your private iCloud account (if enabled)
  • Never sent to external servers or shared with third parties

Linked to your identity: NO - Health data is not linked to your personal identity.

Used for tracking: NO - Health data is never used for tracking purposes.

2.2 What We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location Data: We do not collect or track your location
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts
  • Browsing History: We do not track browsing or search history
  • Usage Analytics: We do not use analytics services to track your usage patterns

2.3 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Your app data is synced across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage
  • Data is encrypted by Apple during transmission and storage
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud-synced data: Apple Privacy Policy

2.4 In-App Purchases

What we collect: Purchase status for "Remove Ads" ($4.99 one-time purchase)

How we use it: To enable the ad-free experience in the app

Where it's stored: All payment processing handled by Apple. We only receive confirmation that the purchase was made.

Linked to your identity: NO - We only know that the app has been unlocked, not who you are.

Used for tracking: NO - Purchase information is used solely for app functionality.

4. Medical Data and HIPAA

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Information for Healthcare Professionals

While CKD-MBD Decision-Maker is designed with HIPAA-conscious principles (local-only data storage, encryption), it is your responsibility as a healthcare professional to ensure your use of the app complies with HIPAA and other applicable regulations in your jurisdiction.

4.1 Data Security Measures

  • Local Storage: All data stored locally on device with iOS encryption
  • No Cloud Transmission: Data never leaves your device (except via optional iCloud sync)
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access your data remotely
  • Device Protection: Protected by your device passcode/biometric authentication

4.2 Your Responsibilities

As a healthcare professional using this app, you should:

  • Secure your device with a strong passcode or biometric authentication
  • Not share your device with unauthorized persons
  • Follow your organization's policies regarding mobile device use
  • Understand that data on a lost/stolen device may be accessible if not properly secured
  • Use device encryption features provided by iOS

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or medical data with anyone.

5.2 Legal Requirements

Since we do not collect or have access to your data, we have no data to disclose to legal authorities. Any data on your device would be subject to legal processes directed at you or Apple (for iCloud data).

6. Your Sharing Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • PDF Export: When you export calculations as PDF, YOU choose where to share them (email, messages, etc.)
  • Data Deletion: Delete patients, calculations, or all data anytime within the app
  • iCloud Control: Enable or disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app deletes all local data

7. Children's Privacy

CKD-MBD Decision-Maker is not directed at children under 13 years of age. The app is designed exclusively for healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. International Users

CKD-MBD Decision-Maker can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, data processing occurs on your device in your location. If you enable iCloud sync, data may be stored in Apple's data centers, subject to Apple's privacy practices and your region's iCloud terms.

9. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • We do not have access to your data, so we cannot delete it for you
  • To completely remove all data, delete the app and (if using iCloud) disable iCloud sync in Settings

10. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app
  • Modification: You can edit patient information and calculations
  • Deletion: You can delete any or all data in the app
  • Export: You can export calculations as PDF
  • Portability: Your data stays with you on your devices

11. Security

11.1 iOS Security Features

The app leverages iOS's built-in security features:

  • iOS keychain for sensitive data
  • Automatic data encryption when device is locked
  • Sandboxed app environment
  • Secure enclave for authentication

11.2 Best Practices

We recommend:

  • Keep your iOS version up to date
  • Use a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Enable "Find My" to remotely wipe device if lost
  • Don't jailbreak your device
