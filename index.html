Professional clinical decision support for healthcare professionals

About

CKD-MBD Decision-Maker is a professional clinical decision support tool designed for healthcare professionals managing Chronic Kidney Disease - Mineral and Bone Disorder (CKD-MBD). Make evidence-based treatment decisions with confidence using KDIGO guidelines.

Key Features

  • Evidence-Based Calculator - Calculate CKD-MBD recommendations based on Calcium, Phosphate, and PTH levels following KDIGO guidelines
  • Patient Management - Create and manage patient profiles with demographics, calculation history, and clinical notes
  • Flexible Unit Systems - Support for both mmol/L and mg/dL measurements
  • Calculation History - Save and track all calculations with timestamps and clinical notes
  • Professional PDF Reports - Generate and share professional PDF reports for clinical documentation
  • Clinical Reference Library - Quick access to lab ranges, medication information, and KDIGO guidelines
  • Trend Analysis - Interactive charts showing lab value trends over time (Premium)
  • iCloud Sync - Automatic data synchronization across all your devices
  • Beautiful Interface - Modern design with dark mode support and full accessibility features

Common Questions

Q: How do I unlock premium features?
A: Tap Settings ‚Üí Premium Upgrade, then follow the in-app purchase flow.

Q: How do I export calculations?
A: Open any saved calculation and tap the "Export as PDF" button to share or save the report.

Q: How do I sync across devices?
A: Enable iCloud in your iOS Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud, and make sure iCloud Drive is enabled. The app will automatically sync your data.

Q: Can I change between mmol/L and mg/dL?
A: Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Unit System and select your preferred measurement system.

Q: Is my patient data secure?
A: Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device and encrypted. If you use iCloud sync, Apple's encrypted storage is used. We never have access to your data.
