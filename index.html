Frequently Asked Questions

What is CKD-MBD Decision-Maker?
CKD-MBD Decision-Maker is a professional clinical decision support tool for healthcare professionals managing chronic kidney disease - mineral and bone disorder (CKD-MBD). It provides KDIGO guideline-based recommendations for calcium, phosphate, PTH, and vitamin D management.
How do I use the calculator?
1. Enter patient lab values (calcium, phosphate, PTH, vitamin D)
2. Select the CKD stage
3. Tap "Calculate" to get treatment recommendations
4. Optionally save to a patient profile for tracking over time

How do I create a patient profile?
1. Go to the Patients tab
2. Tap the + button
3. Enter patient information (only initials required)
4. Calculations can now be saved to this patient's history
Does this app share my data?
No. All patient data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your data. The free version displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Can I export calculations?
Yes! After performing a calculation, tap "Export PDF" to generate a professional report that can be shared via email or saved to files.
How do I sync data across devices?

What are the sources for the recommendations?
All recommendations are based on KDIGO (Kidney Disease: Improving Global Outcomes) 2017 Clinical Practice Guideline Update for CKD-MBD. You can view full citations in the app under Reference â†’ Clinical Citations.
Is this app HIPAA compliant?
The app is designed with HIPAA-conscious principles (local-only data storage, no external data transmission, encryption via iOS). However, HIPAA compliance ultimately depends on how you use the app. Ensure you follow your organization's policies regarding mobile device use.
Can this app prescribe medications?
No. This app does NOT calculate drug dosages or prescribe medications. It provides general treatment recommendations based on lab values (e.g., "Consider phosphate binders" or "May need vitamin D supplementation"). All recommendations must be interpreted in the context of individual patient circumstances and should not replace professional medical judgment.

Troubleshooting

The app won't calculate
Make sure all required fields are filled in (calcium, phosphate, PTH, vitamin D, and CKD stage). Values must be within realistic ranges.
My data isn't syncing
1. Check that iCloud is enabled in iOS Settings
2. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices
3. Check your internet connection
4. Data may take a few moments to sync
Purchase restore not working
Go to Settings â†’ Tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
